About Us

We are the data science team of Project Meta where we programmed a chatbot to help customers navigate the online shopping mall better and receive help without the need for human interaction. Furthermore, we have developed a interactive dashboard that visualizes customer information and behaviour based on the information gathered from chatbot responses and shopping mall activity for managers to devise customer targeted ads/events.

Our project allows you to:

  • Collect sentiment analysis from an AI powered chatbot
  • Analyze customer behaviour and interaction
  • Track online user activity for the website
